DETAILS

Crafts • Relaxation • Coping Strategies • Community • Joy


DESCRIPTION

Our Father's Daughters was founded by a group of ladies brought together by the common thread of tragedy but forever bonded by God's healing touch. In memory of our earthly fathers, we want to follow our Heavenly Father's plan to help other young ladies walk in joy again after losing a parent by offering community, prayers, support, and coping strategies.
